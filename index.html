Important Safety Rules First! (Super Important!)

Grown-Up Helper

You NEED a parent, teacher, or another trusted adult with you. They'll help with tricky words, clicking the right buttons, and making sure everything is safe and okay to use.

Free Stuff vs. Paid Stuff

Freepik has lots of FREE pictures, but some cost money ("Premium"). NEVER click on anything that asks for money or credit card info. Stick to the FREE stuff and let your grown-up helper know if you see anything confusing.

Ads Everywhere!

Websites sometimes have tricky buttons that look like part of the game but are actually ads. Point to the button you want to click and ask your grown-up helper "Is this the right one?" before clicking!

For Grown-Up Helpers

Supervision is Key

Actively monitor the child's browsing. Freepik contains ads and premium content.

Account Consideration

While not strictly necessary for free downloads, creating a free account can manage downloads slightly better (and avoids repeated captchas), but requires an email.

Explain Licensing

Briefly explain the child needs to credit the author if required (many free Freepik items require attribution - check the license info). Keep it simple: "We need to say who drew this picture."

Ad Awareness

Point out common ad placements and teach them not to click anything flashing or saying "Download" except the specific Freepik download button for their chosen free resource.

Folder Management

Help them locate the downloaded file (usually PNG, JPG, or sometimes SVG) in the Downloads folder.
